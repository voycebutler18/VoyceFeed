Snayvu Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Snayvu collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly:

  • **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your email address and password.
  • **Profile Information:** You may choose to provide additional profile details.
  • **User Content:** We collect the content you create and upload to the Service, including videos, descriptions, titles, hashtags, comments, and any associated metadata.
  • **Communications:** We collect information you provide when you communicate with us, such as feedback or support inquiries.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically:

  • **Usage Data:** We collect information about your activity on the Service, such as the videos you watch, your interactions with content, features you use, and the time, frequency, and duration of your activities.
  • **Device Information:** We collect information about the device you use to access the Service, including IP address, device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • **Cookies and Similar Technologies:** We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, authenticate your access, and understand how you use our Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • **To Provide and Maintain the Service:** This includes enabling access to content, processing your uploads, and managing your account.
  • **To Improve and Personalize the Service:** We use your information to understand trends, develop new features, and recommend content relevant to your interests and viewing habits.
  • **To Communicate with You:** We may send you service-related announcements, updates, and support messages.
  • **For Safety and Security:** We use information to detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and other harmful activities, and to maintain the integrity of our platform.
  • **For Analytics:** To understand and analyze how users interact with our Service and identify areas for improvement.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • **With Other Users:** Certain information, like your username and public profile, and your User Content, will be visible to other users of the Service.
  • **Service Providers:** We may share information with third-party vendors and service providers who perform functions on our behalf, such as hosting, analytics, and customer support. These providers are obligated to protect your information.
  • **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • **Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Your Choices and Rights

  • **Account Information:** You can access and update your account information through your account settings.
  • **Content Deletion:** You can delete your uploaded content and comments through the Service.
  • **Cookies:** Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at info@snayvu.com.
