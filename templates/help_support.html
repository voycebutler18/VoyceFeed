Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I upload a video? ▶
To upload a video, log in to your Snayvu account, click on the "Upload" option in the left sidebar menu, and follow the on-screen instructions to select your video file, add a title, description, and hashtags.
How can I discover new content? ▶
You can discover new content through various sections on your homepage, including "Trending Now," "New This Week," and "Emotional Picks." You can also use the "Discover" option in the sidebar to explore tags and creators.
Is Snayvu free to use? ▶
Yes, Snayvu is currently free to use. All content and features are accessible without any subscription fees.
How do I report inappropriate content? ▶
If you encounter content that violates our Terms of Service, please use the reporting features available on the platform or contact our support team directly via the contact information below.
